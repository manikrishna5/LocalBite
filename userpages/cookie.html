Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how FoodHub uses cookies and similar technologies to provide, personalize, and improve our services.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, login details, and improve browsing experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

a) Essential Cookies
Required for the website to work properly — login, navigation, security.

b) Performance Cookies
Help us understand site usage, loading speed, and improve performance.

c) Functional Cookies
Remember your preferences like location, saved addresses, and cart items.

d) Advertising Cookies
Used to show personalized ads based on your interests and order history.

3. Why We Use Cookies

• Improve user experience • Remember login details • Track orders & preferences • Personalize recommendations • Analyze website traffic

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use trusted third-party services like Google Analytics, Ads, and Maps. These providers may place their own cookies for analytics and ads.

6. Updates to This Cookie Policy

FoodHub may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about our Cookie Policy, contact us at privacy@foodhub.com.
