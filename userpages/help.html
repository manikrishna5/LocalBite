Help & Support

Find answers to common questions or reach out to our support team.

How do I track my order?

After placing your order, go to the "Orders" page to view live status and real-time tracking updates.

I received the wrong order. What should I do?

Contact support immediately. We will arrange a replacement or initiate a refund instantly.

How do I change my delivery address?

You can update your address from the “Profile” section before placing an order.

How can I contact the delivery partner?

Once the order is assigned, you will see a call icon on the order tracking screen.

My payment failed but amount deducted.

Refunds typically take 3–5 business days. If not, contact support with your transaction ID.
